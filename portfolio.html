Kumasi Jay Gwynne Photography

Kumasi was looking for a way to showcase his photography online. We decided to use a modern and minimalistic design to match the Kumasi's approach to images. We also worked with Kumasi to gather the images and make them ready to be posted on the website. The website consists of two pages - home page and gallery page. The home page has a button that allows you to go to the gallery page, which houses Kumasi's photos.

Services provided: web design and development, adding watermarks to images, image optimization, making sure the website is responsive (or mobile-friendly)
